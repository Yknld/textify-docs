Textify ("we", "our", or "the extension") is committed to protecting your privacy. This policy explains how we handle your data.

Summary: We only process files you explicitly upload. We don't sell your data. Files are processed and immediately discarded - we don't store your documents.

1. Information We Collect

Account Information:

  • Email address (for authentication)
  • Account balance and transaction history

Usage Data:

  • Number of files processed (for billing)
  • Token usage statistics
  • Device identifier (anonymized, for preventing abuse)

2. Information We Do NOT Collect

  • We do NOT store your uploaded files or screenshots
  • We do NOT store the extracted text content permanently
  • We do NOT track your browsing history
  • We do NOT sell or share your personal data with third parties

3. How We Process Your Files

When you upload a file or capture a screenshot:

  1. The file is sent to our secure backend server
  2. We process it using OpenAI's GPT-4 Vision API for text extraction
  3. The extracted text is returned to you
  4. The original file is immediately discarded - we do not store it

4. Third-Party Services

We use the following trusted third-party services:

  • OpenAI: For AI-powered text extraction (OpenAI Privacy Policy)
  • Supabase: For secure authentication and account management (Supabase Privacy Policy)
  • Stripe: For secure payment processing (Stripe Privacy Policy)

5. Data Security

We implement industry-standard security measures:

  • All data transmission is encrypted via HTTPS
  • Passwords are securely hashed (never stored in plain text)
  • Authentication tokens are stored locally on your device
  • Payment information is handled entirely by Stripe - we never see your card details

6. Local Storage

The extension stores the following data locally on your device:

  • Your authentication session (for staying logged in)
  • Recent extraction history (for your convenience)
  • A device identifier (for preventing signup bonus abuse)

You can clear this data at any time by removing the extension.

7. Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Access your account data
  • Delete your account and all associated data
  • Export your transaction history
  • Opt out of non-essential communications

8. Children's Privacy

Textify is not intended for users under 13 years of age. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children.

9. Changes to This Policy

We may update this privacy policy from time to time. We will notify users of any material changes by updating the "Last updated" date.
