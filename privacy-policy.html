Textify ("we", "us", "our", or "the extension") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains what data we collect, how we use it, how we store it, and how we share it. By using Textify, you agree to the practices described in this policy.

Summary: We only process files you explicitly upload. We don't sell your data. Uploaded files are processed and immediately discarded - we don't permanently store your documents or their contents.

1. Data Collection

We collect the following categories of user data:

1.1 Account Information (Collected)

  • Email address: Required for account creation and authentication
  • Password: Securely hashed, never stored in plain text
  • Account creation date: For record-keeping purposes

1.2 Usage Data (Collected)

  • File processing records: File names, sizes, and types of documents processed (for billing and history features)
  • Token usage: Number of AI tokens consumed (for billing purposes)
  • Transaction history: Records of payments and balance changes
  • Timestamps: When files were processed

1.3 Device Information (Collected)

  • Device identifier: An anonymized identifier to prevent signup bonus abuse

1.4 File Content (Temporarily Processed, NOT Stored)

  • Uploaded files: Images, PDFs, spreadsheets, and documents you upload are processed in memory and immediately discarded
  • Extracted text: The text extracted from your files is returned to you and stored locally in your browser history - we do not retain it on our servers

1.5 Data We Do NOT Collect

  • Browsing history or websites visited
  • Personal files not explicitly uploaded by you
  • Location data
  • Contacts or address book
  • Microphone or camera data (beyond screenshots you explicitly capture)

2. Data Handling and Use

We use collected data for the following purposes:

Data Type Purpose Legal Basis
Email address Account authentication, password reset, important service notifications Contract fulfillment
Usage data Billing, providing extraction history feature, service improvement Contract fulfillment, Legitimate interest
Device identifier Preventing abuse of signup bonuses Legitimate interest
Uploaded files Text extraction (processed immediately, not stored) Contract fulfillment

We do NOT use your data for:

  • Advertising or marketing to third parties
  • Selling to data brokers
  • Training AI models (your files are not used for training)
  • Profiling or automated decision-making that affects you

3. Data Storage

3.1 Where Data is Stored

  • Account data: Stored securely on Supabase servers (cloud infrastructure)
  • Payment data: Stored and processed by Stripe (we never see your full card number)
  • Local data: Authentication tokens and extraction history are stored locally on your device using Chrome's storage API

3.2 Data Retention Periods

Data Type Retention Period
Account information Until you delete your account
Usage/transaction history Until you delete your account
Uploaded files Not retained - processed in memory and immediately discarded
Extracted text Stored locally on your device only - you can clear this anytime
Device identifier Until you delete your account or uninstall the extension

3.3 Data Security Measures

  • All data transmission is encrypted using HTTPS/TLS
  • Passwords are hashed using industry-standard algorithms
  • Authentication tokens are stored securely in browser storage
  • Server infrastructure uses modern security practices
  • Access to user data is restricted to authorized personnel only

5. Cookies and Tracking

Textify does not use cookies for tracking or advertising purposes.

  • Authentication tokens: Stored in browser local storage (not cookies) for keeping you logged in
  • No third-party trackers: We do not embed any analytics, advertising, or tracking scripts
  • No cross-site tracking: We do not track your activity across other websites

6. International Data Transfers

Your data may be processed in countries outside your country of residence, including the United States, where our service providers operate. We ensure appropriate safeguards are in place:

  • Our service providers (OpenAI, Supabase, Stripe) maintain compliance with international data protection standards
  • Data transfers are protected by encryption in transit
